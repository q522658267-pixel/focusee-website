FAQs

What products does FOCUSEE manufacture? +

We manufacture a wide range of home appliances including heaters, fan heaters, fans, electric kettles, mini cookers, food choppers, food cooler pans, induction cookers, rice cookers, and fried ice machines.

Do you offer OEM/ODM services? +

Yes, we provide full OEM/ODM solutions. Our experienced R&D team can work with you to develop custom products tailored to your brand requirements, from design and prototyping to mass production.

What certifications do your products have? +

Our products hold a wide range of international certifications including PSE, KC, BSMI, CE, EMC, LVD, ERP, RoHS, CB, and GS, ensuring compliance with quality standards across global markets.

What is your minimum order quantity? +

MOQ varies by product type and customization level. Generally, our MOQ ranges from 500 to 3,000 units depending on the product. Please contact our sales team for specific MOQ information.

Where is your factory located? +

Our factory is located in ShunDe, Guangdong, China — known as the Capital of China's Household Electrical Appliances. This strategic location gives us access to a mature supply chain and skilled workforce.

What is your production capacity? +

Our facility has a production capacity of over 100,000 units monthly. We can scale production based on order requirements and seasonal demand fluctuations.

What is the typical lead time for orders? +

Standard orders typically take 30-45 days from confirmation. OEM/ODM projects with custom designs may require 45-60 days including sampling and approval phases.

Do you provide product samples? +

Yes, we provide product samples for evaluation. Sample lead time is usually 7-15 days. Please contact our sales team to request samples and discuss your requirements.
